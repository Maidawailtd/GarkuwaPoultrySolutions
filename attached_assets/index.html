Our Livestock

Broiler Chickens

Fast-growing breeds for premium quality meat production, ready in 6-8 weeks.

History & Life Cycle

Broiler chickens have been selectively bred for rapid growth and efficient feed conversion. Their life cycle typically spans 6-8 weeks from hatch to market weight.

History & Life Cycle

Fish farming has a long history as a sustainable food source. The life cycle includes egg, fry, fingerling, and adult stages, with growth periods varying by species.
